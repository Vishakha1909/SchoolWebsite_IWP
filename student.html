 CO-SCHOLASTIC:- Apart from academics, students at VKPS can look forward to loads of fun. With a wide range of co-curricular activities and various sporting events the academic year is never boring! To build leadership skills and understand the value of teamwork, students of Classes V to XII get a unique opportunity to participate in decision-making bodies like the School Cabinet Member Body, Student Council, etc. A broad-based selection policy is in place to enable maximum participation in the various activities.
CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES:- VKPS truly believes that to develop self confidence and self esteem in the children and make them ready to take on the world, extra-curricular activities have to be encouraged and every child must be motivated to participate in them. Club activities are encouraged and every child has to opt for one area of specialisation. A wide spectrum of activities ranging from Yoga, Chess, Quiz, Public Speaking, Instrumental music, Vocal Music, Dance, Calligraphy and several others are offered.
